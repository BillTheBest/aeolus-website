Tools

  • Deltacloud Portal

    Give your users a self-service window into your company's internal and external clouds. Deltacloud Portal provides a user-friendly, cross-platform UI in front of the Deltacloud API. With Deltacloud Portal, your users can manage instances, keep track of available images, migrate workloads from internal to external clouds, and monitor their own cloud resource usage.

  • Deltacloud Proxy

    If you want to build your own cloud UI, Deltacloud Proxy gets you halfway there. Deltacloud Proxy tracks your external and internal cloud accounts and manages user authorization and resource limits; your custom user interface does the rest.
